Model United Nations

I participated in the MUN program to develop my public speaking, negotiation, critical thinking and problem-solving skills. It took place over a period of four months and consisted of five training sessions and the final two-day conference.

  • Represented Egypt on the issue of "Controlling Epidemics in Global Mass Gatherings"
  • Wrote a position paper, delivered many speaches (prepared & imporomptu), and participated in discussion with other student delegates.
  • Drafted a resolution paper and rallied other country representatives to sponsor it.
  • Won the "Diplomacy" award for displaying excellent persuasion, collaboration, and leadership skills
